**Launch Components

Open two terminals and run:

Terminal 1 (gNB):
sudo srsgnb --config-file srsran_setup/gnb.conf

Terminal 2 (UE):
sudo srsue --config-file srsran_setup/ue.conf


If you’re using simulation mode (ZMQ radio), they will connect virtually — no SDR hardware required.

**Telemetry Capture

Logs will be generated in:

~/.config/srsran/ue_logs.txt


These logs include PRB usage, CQI, throughput, and latency — used by your AI scheduler for telemetry extraction.

**Integration with AI Scheduler

After the gNB and UE generate telemetry, run:

python3 src/data_extraction.py --parse ~/.config/srsran/
python3 src/train_rl_model.py
python3 src/ai_scheduler.py
